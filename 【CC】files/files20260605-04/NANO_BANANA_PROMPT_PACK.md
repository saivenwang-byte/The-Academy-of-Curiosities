## 0. 通用风格前缀（STYLE BLOCK · 每条提示词都以它开头）

**EN:**
> Soft watercolor Japanese picture-book (ehon) illustration, gentle and semi-realistic, warm painterly style with visible watercolor texture and soft edges, natural soft light, warm low-saturation palette. Not anime, not chibi, not 3D/CG. Wholesome and age-appropriate. Setting: a Japanese public elementary school in Nagoya. No text, no logos, no scary or supernatural elements, no modern Chinese or Western school elements.

**ZH:**
> 柔和水彩日本绘本风插画，温柔、半写实，温暖而有水彩纹理与柔边，自然柔光，暖色低饱和。非动漫、非萌系、非3D。健康、适龄。场景为名古屋的日本市立小学。画面内无文字、无logo、无恐怖或超自然元素、无中式或西式校园元素。

## 4. 单案场景模板（套用任意卷/案）

> STYLE BLOCK + 一致性指令 + 下面模板（按案件卡填空）。内文跨页用 **3:2 横**。

**EN:** [TIME/SEASON, matching the case month] in [LOCATION at the Nagoya school]. [WHICH CHARACTERS] are [ACTION tied to the case]. The scene quietly shows [DEPICTABLE FAIR CLUE(S) for this case] without revealing the explanation. [LIGHT/MOOD]. [COMPOSITION/FRAMING]. Landscape 3:2.

**ZH:** 〔与卷次月份吻合的时间/季节〕，地点在〔名古屋校园的具体场所〕。〔出场角色〕正在〔与本案相关的动作〕。画面安静地呈现〔本案可入画的公平线索〕但不剧透答案。〔光线/情绪〕。〔构图/景别〕。横幅3:2。

**示例（案①《湿椅子》关键场景）EN:** Early April morning in the 5年2組 classroom, by the window row. Hikaru and Keimi lean in to look at one wooden chair whose seat is faintly damp, while every other chair nearby is dry; the inner window glass is lightly fogged and the metal window frame looks cold; Shun crouches at the chair's window side, pointing at where its metal frame presses against the window frame with no gap. Kei watches and writes. Soft cool morning light, gentle and curious mood. Landscape 3:2.

**示例 ZH：** 四月清晨的五年二组教室，靠窗一列。光和慧美凑近看一把座面微微发潮的木椅，而旁边其它椅子都是干的；窗玻璃内侧蒙着一层薄雾，金属窗框显得冰冷；陸珣蹲在椅子靠窗一侧，指着它的金属框与窗框严丝合缝、没有缝隙的地方。陸瑆在一旁边看边记。清冷柔和的晨光，温柔好奇的氛围。横幅3:2。
